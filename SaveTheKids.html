Save The Kids 

            In 1991, Micheal Servor was introduced to Java.
            He liked how he could make simple and complex
            programs using Java.
        
            And few years later. In 1995, JavaScript was introduced to the public
            Micheal Servor was anxious that JavaScript would take over
            Java as the best programming language.
        
            To make sure that everyone in his town know
            what Java is. Micheal Servor kiddnapped 10 kids.
            Word got to the town people that the only way to 
            get the kids back is by proving to Micheal Servor
            that you know your Java.
